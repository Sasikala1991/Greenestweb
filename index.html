FROM AIR PORT

From air port, reach small signal before Vyttila main signal and take right. After Ponnurunni Railway over bridge only 600 mtrs to Hotel Greenest & Homemates Super market ( Right side).

FROM TRIVANDRUM / ALLEPPEY..

From Trivandrum side, cross Vyttila main signal and take left from the small signal... After Ponnurunni Railway over bridge only 600 mtrs to Hotel Greenest & Homemates Super market ( Right side).

FROM ERNAKULAM NORTH..

From Ernakulam North side (Menaka, Kacherppady, kaloor) get in to International Stadium, Kaloor and reach Thammanam jn. Take right and after Kuthappady Ayyappa Temple, Greenest hotel & Homemates supermarkets are located in the left side of the road.

FROM ERNAKULAM SOUTH..

From Ernakulam South side (Railway station, Pallimukku, Kadavanthra) take left from Vyttila main signal and again take left from the small signal... After Ponnurunni Railway over bridgeonly 600 mtrs to Hotel Greenest & Homemates Super market ( Right side).
